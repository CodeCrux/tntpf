About

Welcome to Tamilnadu Telugu People Foundation



Tamilnadu Telugu People Foundation

“There is no greater joy, nor greater reward than to make a fundamental difference in someone’s life.”

Vision

Tamilnadu Telugu People Foundation intends to address the needs of these underprivileged people who are suffering from COVID19 and have nothing in hand.

Mission

Tamilnadu Telugu People Foundation further intends to bring a lasting change in their lives by way of providing support, counseling, encouragement and a healthy environment.

Support

Tamilnadu Telugu People Foundation by its small effort would help them attain the right to survival, development and participation in the mainstream of the society.
